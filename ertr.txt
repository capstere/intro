PQC Rework Plan instruction:

Due to changed market demand, a portion of GXSACOMP-CE-10 Lot 19003 / Batch 1001528736 is reworked to GXSACOMP-JP-10, Batch 1001534891. The rework consists of unpacking kits, sorting contents, relabeling cartridges, and repacking on PAL.

The cartridges originate from the same lot (Lot 19003). Part number (700-4023) and ADF (P0358) remain unchanged. The rework does not impact cartridge design, assay chemistry, ADF, or LSP, and therefore does not affect intrinsic product performance characteristics.

Approved same-lot source data from the original D36872 worksheet will be used as source evidence for the JP release package for:
- First 10 / Sample Type 0: 10 replicates
- Sample Type 0 Negative: 100 replicates
- Sample Type 1 Medium Positive: 70 replicates

A JP-specific testing gap has been identified consisting of:

- Sample Type 3 High Positive: 10 replicates

The final D66614 worksheet shall clearly distinguish:
- approved original same-lot source data
- newly generated post-rework data

Original source data shall not be considered newly generated JP test data but shall be used as approved same-lot source evidence. Any accepted errors or minor failures present in the original data remain part of the final assessment and shall not be removed, repeated, or reset.

Final release is acceptable only if the completed D66614 worksheet, including both original source data and additional post-rework data, meets all applicable acceptance criteria.
